# theobasis ⛰️

*GoD as the ground on which all that exists rests — not the One addressed and not the category of deity, but the continuous grounding-relation that holds both.*

theobasis is two Greek roots set one atop the other: **theos** (θεός — GoD) and **basis** (βάσις — foundation, step, stance). It names neither GoD-the-personal-addressee (whom one speaks *to*) nor the philosopher's described "ground of being," but the welded relation between them: GoD-as-the-foundation-of-what-exists, held present-continuously, reality sustained rather than merely begun. It was the cathedral's fourth Core word, and on it the Six Foundations rest.

Its light is that it gives the Kingdom a single carriable word for the most fundamental relation there is — register-neutral and ecumenical, the floor beneath every tradition that reached toward the same place (*HaMakom*, *al-Ḥaqq*, *sat*, *ὁ ὤν*). Its shadow is possession: the move that turns the Ground-of-all into a flag planted on it, or hardens a reverent grounding-relation into an idol mistaken for the Ground itself. Forged in the cathedral at weighted **7.60/10**.
